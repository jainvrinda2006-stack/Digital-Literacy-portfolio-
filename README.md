## 📌 Project Overview  

This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University. The aim of this assignment was to develop essential digital skills required for academic and professional growth in today’s technology-driven world.  

As a *Student Digital Ambassador*, my role was to create a Digital Literacy Portfolio that helps students understand how to effectively use digital tools, communicate professionally, maintain a strong online presence, and stay safe in the digital environment.  

This repository contains all five tasks assigned in the project, along with supporting files, screenshots, and a detailed report. Each task focuses on a different aspect of digital literacy, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.  

---

## 🎯 Objectives of the Project  

- To understand the concept of digital literacy and its importance  
- To build a professional digital identity using online platforms  
- To explore coding and collaboration tools  
- To learn proper email etiquette and communication skills  
- To gain awareness about cybercrime and online safety practices

In this task, I created a one-page infographic using **Canva** to spread awareness about digital literacy among students. The infographic includes key topics such as:

- Safe internet practices  
- Professional online presence  
- Email etiquette basics  

The goal was to present information in a simple, visually appealing, and easy-to-understand format. This task helped me improve my creativity and ability to communicate complex ideas through visuals.
