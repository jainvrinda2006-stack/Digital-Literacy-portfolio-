
# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

##  Do’s

* Think before you post — ask yourself if it’s something you’d be okay with everyone seeing.
* Keep your profiles private and secure to protect your personal information.
* Use social media to learn new things, connect with people, and grow professionally.
* Be kind and respectful in your comments and messages.
* Double-check information before sharing it with others.

##  Don’ts

* Don’t share personal details like your address, phone number, or passwords.
* Don’t be part of bullying, trolling, or spreading negativity online.
* Don’t post anything inappropriate that could affect your image in the future.
* Don’t forward or believe everything you see without verifying it.
* Don’t spend too much time scrolling and neglect your studies or important work.
