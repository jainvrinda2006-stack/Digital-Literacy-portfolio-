# Stay Safe Online – Simple Checklist

##  General Safety Tips

* Use strong passwords and don’t keep the same one for every account.
* Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra protection.
* Don’t click on random links you get in messages or emails—they can be risky.
* Try to avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi.
* Keep your phone and apps updated so they stay secure.
* Think twice before posting personal details, photos, or your location online.
* Only download apps from trusted platforms like the Play Store or App Store.

##  UPI / Payment Safety

* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—no matter who they claim to be.
* Remember: you don’t need to enter your PIN to receive money.
* Always check payment requests carefully before approving them.
* Don’t scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.

##  If Something Goes Wrong

* Report any cyber fraud immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* You can also call the helpline number 1930 for quick help.
